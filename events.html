CS Night 21-22

CS Night is an event dedicated to promoting computer science to younger grades. Students from Reed Intermediate School were invited to the high school to learn about CS through various activities, from playing with interactive robots to coloring pictures pixel by pixel as a computer would do. Through this, kids are able to engage with CS, hopefully choosing to pursue it further in the future. Click the image to the right for more!
