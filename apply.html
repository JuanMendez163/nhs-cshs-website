Prerequisites

  • Be a student in 10th, 11th, or 12th grade
  • Attend Newtown High School for at least one semester
  • Demonstrate service and leadership
  • Complete at least one semester of computer science coursework and earn at least a B, 85%
  • Enroll in or complete a second semester of computer science coursework and earn at least a B, 85%
